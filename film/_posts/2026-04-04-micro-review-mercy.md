The story and the idea behind it are so good, but the screenplay falls apart so quickly.

I couldn't help but think how this movie should have been a game. An interactive experience with an 8+ hour timer and a bunch of explorable menus would have done this concept justice. Makes me wonder if someone could write a custom storyline as a *Hacknet* [Extension](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=914587661) using the "prove your innocence" idea.

**5/10 ★★★★★☆☆☆☆☆**
